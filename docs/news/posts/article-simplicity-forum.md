# Simplicity Community forum launched

Simplicity and SimplicityHL now have a [Simplicity Community](https://community.simplicity-lang.org/) web forum, hosted by Discourse. The Simplicity Community will share Blockstream's own Simplicity work, as well as supporting Simplicity users and providing a place for them to share their projects and applications.

\- Blockstream Team
